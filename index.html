01. About Me

I'm a Mathematician turned CTO with 10+ years of experience leading engineering teams and building platforms from zero to scale. My path started with pattern recognition research in biometrics at CENATAV, and evolved through co-founding startups, architecting multi-tenant SaaS platforms, and now leading technology strategy at Coverfleet.

I believe the best engineering comes from first-principles thinking — the same rigor you apply to a topology proof can design a bulletproof distributed system. I'm passionate about clean architecture, developer experience, and building tools that make teams move faster.

When I'm not architecting systems, you'll find me exploring agentic AI patterns, writing about engineering practices, contributing to open source, or deep-diving into distributed systems.

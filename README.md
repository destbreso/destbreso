### 👋 About Me

I'm a **Mathematician turned CTO** — I started in pattern recognition research at CENATAV's biometrics lab, co-founded startups, and now lead technology strategy at **[Coverfleet](https://coverfleet.com)**. With 10+ years building platforms from zero to scale, I'm obsessed with clean architecture, first-principles thinking, and engineering tools that make teams move faster.

> *"The same rigor you apply to a topology proof can design a bulletproof distributed system."*

📍 Miami, FL &nbsp;·&nbsp; 🎓 B.Sc. Mathematics, Universidad de La Habana &nbsp;·&nbsp; 🗣 English & Spanish
